About Us

Op. Dr. Nihat Kaya

Nihat Kaya was born in Batman in 1982. He completed his education until high school in Batman. After his medical education in Istanbul Faculty of Medicine between 2001-2007, he completed his Ear Nose and Throat residency in Istanbul Cerrahpaşa Medical Faculty between 2008-2013. After completing his 13-month compulsory service duty in Doğubayazıt district of Ağrı, he performed his profession at Private Batı Bahat Hospital in Istanbul between 2014-2015 and at Yunus Emre Surgical Medical Center as part-time in the first half of 2016.

From 2016 to the beginning of 2020, he accepted his patients in his private clinic in Bakırköy, Istanbul. NK Clinic has been serving in Şişli / Valikonağı since 2020. Since 2015, he has been performing rhinoplasty operations with Piezo (Ultrasonic) Surgery. Rhinoplasty operations with Piezo which minimizes the bruising and swelling that occur especially around the eyes and allows the nasal bones to be shaped in a controlled manner.

Op. Dr. Nihat Kaya has been closely interested in rhinoplasty since the second year of his specialty. He has participated in many congresses on rhinoplasty and has recently spoken as a speaker on "Reshaping the nasal dorsum after Piezo Surgery". Nowadays, he closely follows all innovations related to rhinoplasty.
